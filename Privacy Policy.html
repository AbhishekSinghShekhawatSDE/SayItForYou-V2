Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines how SayItForYou ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe and transparent experience.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At SayItForYou, our core mission is to provide a safe space for you to express your unspoken feelings. We understand the importance of privacy in this process and have designed our service with your anonymity as a top priority. This policy is written in simple terms to clearly explain our practices.

Information We Collect

To provide our service, we need to collect a limited amount of information. We only collect what is essential to deliver your message and maintain the integrity of our platform. When you submit a message through our form, we collect the following:

  • Your Name and Instagram Handle: We use this for verification and to communicate with you if there are any issues with your submission. This information is for internal use only and is never shared with the recipient if you choose to remain anonymous.
  • Your Anonymity Choice: You can choose whether to remain anonymous or have your identity revealed to the recipient. We will strictly adhere to your choice.
  • Message Type and Content: This includes the type of message you're sending (e.g., apology, confession) and the message itself.
  • Recipient‚Äôs Instagram Handle: This is necessary to ensure your message is delivered to the correct person.
  • Additional Information (Optional): You may provide extra context or backstory for us to better understand the situation. This information is for our internal use only and will not be delivered with the message.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information beyond what you voluntarily provide in the submission form. We do not use cookies for tracking purposes.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To Deliver Your Message: We use the recipient's Instagram handle and the message content to create and deliver the message as you have requested.
  • To Improve Our Service: We may analyze the types of messages sent to understand trends and improve the user experience. This analysis is done on an aggregated and anonymized basis.
  • To Communicate With You: We may use your Instagram handle to contact you regarding your submission if necessary.
  • To Maintain a Safe Platform: We review messages to prevent the delivery of content that is hateful, harassing, or otherwise violates our content policy.

Who We Share Your Information With

Your privacy is our priority. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. The only instances in which your information might be shared are:

  • With the Recipient (If You Choose): If you select the option to reveal your identity, your name and/or Instagram handle will be included in the message.
  • With Our Team: Our small, trusted team has access to the information you provide in order to create and deliver your message. They are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect and defend our rights or property, or protect the personal safety of users or the public.

How We Protect Your Information

We take the security of your information seriously. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you submit a message. All information you provide is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology through our form handler.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Choose Anonymity: The core of our service is your ability to send messages anonymously.
  • Request Deletion of Your Data: You can contact us to request the deletion of the personal information you have submitted.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us. You can reach out to us via our Instagram page, @sayitforyou.fun.
