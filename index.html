Questions

Everything you
need to know.

Your trust is the foundation of this service.

Is this free?
Yes, completely free. SayItForYou is a passion project. We love helping people connect.
Will I stay anonymous?
If you choose anonymous, your name and handle will never appear anywhere. Not in the reel, not in the DM. Firm promise.
How long does delivery take?
We handle every message manually. Delivery takes 24 to 48 hours. Real people, real care.
Do you ask the recipient first?
Yes. We DM the recipient and ask if they consent to receive your message. If they agree, we deliver. If they decline, we let you know and nothing gets posted.
What messages do you refuse?
Hate speech, harassment, threats, or harmful content. Every submission is manually reviewed. This service exists only for genuine, positive expression.
